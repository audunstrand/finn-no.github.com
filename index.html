Open Source at FINN.no
We love open source and all of our products are built using vast amounts of open source technology. Therefore it is only fitting that we try to give back by making some of the things we make available to the world. Open source is all about working together and we'd love to have your issues, pull requests or comments on the projects we have made available!
In the spirit of Open Source, we have also made our Open Source Policy available online.
